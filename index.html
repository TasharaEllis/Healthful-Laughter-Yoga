About

My Laughter Journey

Beverly Hamilton, Certified Laughter Yoga Leader (CLYL), established Healthful Laughter Yoga as a tool for sharing positive, life changing experiences with children, seniors and the general public. For many years, I have entertained diverse groups of people as a Clown, Costume Character and a Puppeteer. I am highly skilled in Event Planning, Floral Design and Gift Basket Design. All these experiences add to my passion for encouraging people to smile and laugh. I believe that Laughter Yoga brings out the happiness and joy that is already within you.

What is Laughter Yoga?

Laughter Yoga is a fun interactive activity combined with intentional laughter, yogic breathing, eye contact, gentle movements and a playful attitude.

Some of the areas Laughter Yoga can provide benefits to your life are:

Health

Helps reduce stress, depression and lowers blood pressure.

Personal

Releases endorphins in the brain that changes your mood.

Business

Helps motivate the workforce which includes a strong team building element.

Social

Provides a network of people to connect to for emotional support.
